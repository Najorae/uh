Video Games !


Since I was young I have been playing video games with my mom. I used to sneak and watch her play Heavenly Sword, God of War, and Lara Croft among others, prompting me to climb around the house pretending to be Lara.. I love Skyrim, Hellblade, Life is Strange, Borderlands, The Last of Us, Genshin Impact, Little Nightmares, Folklore, Overcooked, FNF, Child of Eden, etc.

Eventually I grew an interest in the development and production of video games so I made my own game on scratch a few years ago while learning how to use the platform. It was really fun and about my sisters cats and I ended up doing two workshops about the process with groups of kids. This got me to gain more of an interest in coding and computer science. I taught myself C# and hope to use it to make games on Unity now that I have time!
